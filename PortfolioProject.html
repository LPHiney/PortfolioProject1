Getting Loopy

Have you always wanted to make your own earrings, but don't know how?

This class is for you!

Simple loops with head pins, chain and crystals

Treat yourself to a relaxing and fun afternoon creating jewels to enjoy yourself or give to family and friends.

In this workshop, you'll make three (or more) pairs of earrings of your own design as you learn how to create simple, wrapped, and briolette loops.

Saturday, May 1, 2021

12:00 - 4:00 pm

Sparkle Plenty's Bead Store

$50 plus materials

Seats: 6 (due to social distancing)

Wrapped loops with head pins, chain, wire and crystals

Briolette loops with wire and crystals

Come with your own designs or use the ideas included here.

Everyone will learn and practice all three techniques.

Small class size means lots of individual attention and support.

Simple loops with head pins and crystals

Wrapped loops with head pins, pearls and crystals

Briolette loops with wire, coral and crystals

Materials:

  • 22 gauge headpins
  • 24 gauge headpins
  • 22 gauge wire
  • 24 gauge wire
  • Ear wires
  • Assortment of beads
  • At least two briolettes

Tools:

  • Chain-nosed pliers
  • Round-nosed pliers
  • Bent-nosed pliers
  • Wire straightener

Optional:

  • Bead mat
  • Portable light
  • Magnifier
  • Ruler
  • Pen

Availability:

All materials are available at Sparkle Plenty's.

Pliers will be available for your use during class.
